Twine Project

In Ant (2024), created using Twine, the interactive story allows viewers to shape the narrative through their choices. By exploring the contrast between user agency and fixed narrative structure, this work exemplifies the theme of Creative Juxtapositions. The viewer's choices, though seemingly powerful, still operate within the confines of the story’s structure, highlighting the tension between freedom and constraint. The use of interactive media contrasts traditional storytelling, offering a new experience each time the story is engaged with. The dynamic nature of interaction creates an engaging dialogue between the viewer and the work, challenging the boundaries of narrative control.
