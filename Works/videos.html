Ballerina & Pantera Video

In Ballerina & Pantera: Clashing Rhythms (2024), I juxtapose the grace of ballet with the intensity of Pantera's Cowboys from Hell, creating an unexpected yet captivating contrast. Using Premiere Pro, I paired the fluidity of dance with the aggressive rhythm of heavy metal music to explore how two distinct art forms can challenge one another and still coexist. The clash between softness and power speaks to the exhibition's theme of Creative Juxtapositions, encouraging viewers to explore how opposing forces can be harmonized to produce something greater than their individual parts.
