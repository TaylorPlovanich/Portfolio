Mario Kart Blue Shell

is a reinterpretation of the iconic blue shell from the Mario Kart series, created entirely using Adobe Illustrator. In this piece, I focused on reimagining the blue shell, emphasizing clean lines, bold shapes, and sharp details to bring a fresh perspective to a well-known video game symbol. The design process involved illustrating the blue shell from scratch, carefully adjusting its proportions and adding depth with gradients and shading techniques to give it a modern, dynamic look. This work plays with the concept of blending nostalgia with contemporary digital art, using Illustrator's vector tools to create a crisp, stylized version of the shell. By recontextualizing a childhood favorite, the piece invites the viewer to explore how familiar cultural icons can be transformed through modern design.
