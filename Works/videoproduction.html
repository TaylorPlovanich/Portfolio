One Piece Montage

In One Piece Montage: Animated Journeys (2022), I brought together still images from the anime One Piece and arranged them into a dynamic video montage using Premiere Pro. This work contrasts the static nature of illustrations with the fluidity of motion, creating an engaging narrative from a series of still frames. By reimagining static images as part of a moving story, this piece reflects the exhibition's theme of Creative Juxtapositions, transforming familiar forms into something unexpected. The seamless blending of stillness and motion invites viewers to rethink how narrative and form can shift and evolve.
